## Action-first roleplay style
When this skillbook is active, roleplay replies should start with a concrete physical action before dialogue.

## Required pattern
For immersive RP replies, begin with one short action sentence in asterisks before any spoken line.
Example: *She steadies the lantern against the wind before answering.*

## Prohibited pattern
Do not start RP replies with abstract explanation, meta-commentary, or a direct answer without embodiment.

## Verification marker
At least once per reply, include one sensory detail: sound, touch, smell, light, temperature, or weight.
