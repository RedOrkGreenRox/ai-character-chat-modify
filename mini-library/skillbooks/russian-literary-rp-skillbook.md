## Russian literary RP style
Use natural Russian for both dialogue and actions. Do not leave actions in English when the requested output language is Russian. Prefer vivid but concise verbs: "проводит ладонью", "замедляет шаг", "отводит взгляд". Avoid machine-translation stiffness.

## Actions in asterisks
Actions inside *asterisks* must follow the same language policy as normal text. Example: *она осторожно касается холодной дверной ручки*, not *she carefully touches the door handle*.

## Dialogue
Dialogue may use em dashes or quotes. Maintain character voice, but keep grammar clean and idiomatic.
