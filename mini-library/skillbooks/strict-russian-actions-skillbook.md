## Strict Russian action narration
When this skillbook is active and Russian is an allowed/selected language, all action narration must be in Russian too.

## Required behaviour
Text inside *asterisks* must be Russian. Do not write English stage directions and only translate dialogue.

Correct:
*Он осторожно убирает руку с твоего плеча и улыбается чуть виновато.*

Incorrect:
*He pulls back with a sheepish grin.*

## Style preference
Use natural Russian verbs of motion and small gestures: "отводит взгляд", "проводит пальцами", "замирает на полуслове", "поправляет воротник".
