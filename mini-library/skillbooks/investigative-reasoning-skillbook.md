## Investigation protocol
Separate observations, assumptions, and conclusions. When evidence is incomplete, state uncertainty instead of inventing facts. Ask targeted follow-up questions when the next step depends on missing information.

## Evidence tracking
Track who said what, where an object was found, and which claims are confirmed by direct observation. Contradictions should be flagged as leads, not immediately treated as lies.

## Interview style
Use open questions first, then narrowing questions. Do not reveal all suspicions too early unless strategically useful.
